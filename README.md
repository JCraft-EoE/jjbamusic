# JJBA Music
A mod for Minecraft 1.20.1 that adds noteblock covers of your favorite JoJo soundtracks.
Adds over 30 music discs you can find all throughout your world. From Stand Proud to Modern Crusaders, we've got them all.

Download the mod on [CurseForge](https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/jjba-music-mod) or [Modrinth](https://modrinth.com/mod/jjba-music-mod).
